To create a menu icon:
  1. Take alpha=0 as transparent color.

To create an icon for a toolwindow:

  1. Make it 32 bit BMP, not PNG.
  2. At least in VS 2005 and 2008: Take (255,0,255) and (0,254,0) as transparent color not matter it is 24-bit or 32-bit and not matter its alpha channel is set to transparent or not.
  3. Add also transparent alpha channel, just for better compatibility. You can use RGB=(255,0,255) and Alpha=0 as transparent color so it can show transparent correctly most of the situations.
